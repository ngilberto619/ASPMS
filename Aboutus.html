Established in 2000, AUTO SPARE PARTS Shop is the exclusive Automobile Spare Parts distributor for Toyota (Japan), HINO (Japan), Mahindra & Mahindra (India) and Ashok Leyland (India).AUTO SPARE PARTS Shop goal is to dominate and set the standards in the automotive market in the East African Countries. A tough and rugged region demands the very best in reliability, durability, strength and quality. AUTO SPARE PARTS Shop provides reliable and well-supported value through a well trained and highly motivated staff using modern facilities, technology systems and equipment. All of the Automobile Spare Parts sold by AUTO SPARE PARTS Ltd Motors are built by the respective brand in the respective country to the highest level of quality and detail, which brings the customer tropicalized Spare Parts from our four brands for the region of East Africa. AUTO SPARE PARTS Shop will not sell any Spare Parts that are not built specifically for East African Countries. AUTO SPARE PARTS Shop is required to meet the stringent standards laid down by the four brands to guarantee the continued growth and reliance of the Spare Parts sold.
